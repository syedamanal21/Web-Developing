    • Tips

    1. Eat a Balanced Diet:Focus on whole, unprocessed foods. Fill your diet with fruits, vegetables, legumes, nuts, and whole grains. Limit your intake of added sugars and keep your salt intake to around 5 grams a day (about one teaspoon).

    2. Stay Hydrated:Drink plenty of water throughout the day to avoid dehydration. Aim for 6 to 8 glasses daily, depending on your activity level and climate.

    3. Move Your Body Regularly:Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity most days of the week. Incorporate activities you enjoy, such as walking, cycling, swimming, or strength training.

    4. Prioritize Quality Sleep:Adults should aim for 7 to 9 hours of uninterrupted sleep each night. Set a relaxing pre-sleep routine and limit screen time before bed.
