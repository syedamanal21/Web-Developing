School Activities

Debate Competition

A debate competition helps students improve their speaking, critical thinking, and confidence. Students discuss topics from different viewpoints and learn how to express their ideas clearly.

Science Fair

In a science fair, students create projects and experiments to demonstrate scientific concepts. It encourages creativity, innovation, and practical learning.

Sports Day

Sports Day includes various games and athletic events. It promotes physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship among students.

Art Competition

An art competition allows students to showcase their creativity through drawing, painting, or crafts. It helps develop imagination, artistic skills, and self-expression while building confidence in their talents.
