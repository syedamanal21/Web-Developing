Rocket

A rocket is a vehicle, missile, or aircraft that obtains thrust from a rocket engine. Unlike jet engines, rocket engines carry their own oxidizer alongside fuel.

Astronaut

An astronaut is an individual trained by a human spaceflight program to command, pilot, or serve as a crew member aboard a spacecraft

Space

Outer space is the vast, nearly airless expanse that begins about 100 kilometers (62 miles) above Earth at the Kármán line.
