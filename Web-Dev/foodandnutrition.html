Building a Healthy Plate

Why a Balanced Meal Matters

A healthy plate gives our body the energy it needs to study, play, think, and grow. It should include colourful fruits, fresh vegetables, grains, and protein-rich food.

Smart Snack Choices

Snacks can be healthy when we choose foods that give energy instead of only sugar. Fruits, nuts, sprouts, and yoghurt are better choices than chips and sugary drinks.

Nutrition Tips for Students

Drink Enough Water

Water helps the body stay active, supports digestion, and keeps the brain focused. Students should carry a water bottle and drink water throughout the day.

Eat Colourful Food

Different colours in fruits and vegetables give different nutrients. A colourful plate often means a healthier plate.

Quick Nutrition Note

Try this simple rule: fill half your plate with fruits and vegetables, one quarter with grains, and one quarter with protein.
